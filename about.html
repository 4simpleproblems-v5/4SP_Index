About 4SP Index

Connecting students to decentralized, local-first tools.

Inspiration

The 4SP Index was heavily inspired by the original UBGHub. UBGHub pioneered the concept of a central hub for unblocked games and utilities, showing us what was possible with community-driven platforms.

While UBGHub utilizes Supabase, we have built the 4SP Index on Firebase to provide a seamless, secure, and robust experience for our users, adding unique features like card editing for creators and deeper community integration.

Our Mission

To provide a secure, unblockable, and offline-capable toolkit for students. We believe in "Simple as a Standard," avoiding corporate bloat and tracking in favor of speed, privacy, and utility.
